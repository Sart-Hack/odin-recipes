Dhokla Recipe

Description

This Khaman Dhokla recipe makes a wonderfully soft and fluffy, lightly s weet and savory cake that’s perfect to enjoy anytime of the day. It’s easy to prepare Khaman recipe steamed on the stovetop or even in the Instant Pot with my step-by-step photos, video and instructions.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups gram flour (besan), 180 grams
  • 1 tablespoon rava (or semolina) – optional
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1.5 inch ginger crushed to a fine paste in a mortar-pestle
  • 1.5 teaspoons chopped green chillies crushed to a fine paste in a mortar-pestle

Steps

  1. Grease a steamer pan with 2 to 3 teaspoons of oil.
  2. Take besan or gram flour in a mixing bowl or pan.
  3. Add turmeric powder, asafoetida, lemon juice, ginger paste, green chilies paste, sugar, oil and salt.
  4. Stir and then add the rava or sooji (semolina).
  5. Pour the batter in the greased pan.
